Lobster

Description

Steamed lobster tails with a hint of beer. Goes great with melted butter, lemon juice and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 2 whole lobster tail
  • 1/2 (12 fluid once) can beer

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, over medium heat, bring the beer to a boil.
  2. If lobster tails are still in the shell, split the shell lengthwise first.
  3. Place a stteamer basket on top of the saucepan. Place thawed lobster tails in basket and cover. Reduce heat and simmer for 8 minutes.
